# Digital-Apostille-Stamp-Guide
A Digital Apostille Stamp is an electronic authentication certificate issued by a competent government authority to verify the authenticity of public documents for international use. It is recognized by countries that are members of the Hague Apostille Convention and provides a secure, efficient, and verifiable alternative to traditional paper

Information Included in a Digital Apostille

A Digital Apostille generally contains:

Country issuing the Apostille
Competent Authority details
Official electronic signature
Government digital seal
Date of issue
Place of certification
Unique Apostille identification number
QR Code or online verification link
Document authentication details

These security features enable quick and reliable verification by universities, employers, immigration authorities, and government organizations worldwide.
